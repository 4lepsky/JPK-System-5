About Us 

Introduction on the Organization of the Society 



 

 

Profile

JPK or Jawatankuasa Perwakilan Kolej (JPK) is a student representative committee formed to assist university management in overseeing college students. The responsibilities of JPK include planning college activities, disseminating information, taking care of student welfare, and serving as a liaison between students and university management. To support these roles efficiently, the Sistem Pengurusan JPK was developed using a free online platform. In the context of increasing digital transformation in educational institutions, this system provides an organized and accessible platform that enhances the effectiveness of college representative bodies. It automates routine tasks and centralizes data management, reducing administrative burdens and allowing committee members to focus on strategic initiatives and student engagement. The system's commercial value lies in its marketability and profitability, as it can be customized and scaled for various educational institutions. Its modular design allows easy integration with existing platforms, making it attractive for institutions looking to upgrade their management processes. This innovation addresses current challenges faced by college committees and sets a new standard for student governance in the digital age.
